Looking for a volunteering opportunity?

#SGVolunteer is the open-platform for any SG citizens to look for a volunteer opportunities. We only enroll organisations with a strong social causes, albeit all types and sizes, and allow them to administratively run this platform with ease to promote volunteerism. We are confident in providing you with all types of opportunities and we look forward to working with you!

Volunteer opportunities

Have a volunteering opportunity for others?

#SGVolunteer is quick with the enrollment process - simply register on our webpage and we'll do the auditing offline. We have a dedicated team armed with a database of charity organisations and we are confident in providing a swift registration process. Once you've been enrolled as our administrator, you have the full-authority to manage the volunteer opportunities along with other administrators.
