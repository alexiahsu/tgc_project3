#SGVolunteer

We strongly believe that charity comes from within.

Being a first-world country, Singapore's underprivileged and less-fortunate citizens are often masked by media representations of Singapore's prosperity and thus cast aside. As one of the most expensive cities to live in, coupled with wealth inequalities, many citizens often fall through the cracks of government aid.

We have created this platform for anyone with a good heart to co-run this webpage with us. We hope to be the place where any Singaporeans who wish to contribute to society could come to and support our friends.
